--THE RPG GAME--

For the game, you can move using the arrow keys and interact with items using the z key. Alternatively, you can move by clicking using the mouse.
The first level is not a trick. Read the sign.

Level 2 is impossible without cheating. How can you go through the chickens?
Have you missed any puzzles?

There's a puzzle on the 'About' page. Have you solved it yet?

On the 'About Page', Right Click > Inspect.
Under 'elements' there are comments, it tells you about "$gamePlayer.setThrough(true)".
On the game, the solution for Level 1 is to have your character start at the beginning of the dirt path, then walk on the dirt path without ever stepping on the grass. At the end, interact with the cat without stepping off the path.
During Level 2, Right Click > Inspect. Go to the console tab (next to elements), type "$gamePlayer.setThrough(true)" in the console, then press enter. Your character will now be able to walk through walls.

Entering the chicken house is optional, but it does provide a hint to solve Level 3.
To enter the chicken house, you can press 'z' while standing in front of the door.
To get the hint, you can press 'z' in front of the rotating crystal on the top-right.

The are stairs on the top-right of level 2 if you've missed them.
If all you see is a white screen on level 3, keep walking upwards.

"ISGHTOAWNIUMDZ" is a vigenere cipher.
The key is not in this game. (Refer to HINT 11)

The key is "CENTROID" from the "Choose Your Own Adventure" Ending.


--ENDING--
